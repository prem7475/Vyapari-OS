# Vyapari OS Financial Projections

## Executive Summary

Vyapari OS financial projections demonstrate strong growth potential with clear path to profitability. Our conservative projections show ₹120 Cr revenue by Year 3 with 25% EBITDA margins, driven by India's massive SME market opportunity and our first-mover advantage in AI-powered business automation.

**Key Financial Highlights:**
- Year 1 Revenue: ₹15 Cr | Year 3 Revenue: ₹120 Cr
- Break-even: Month 12 | Positive Cash Flow: Year 2
- Gross Margins: 75% | EBITDA Margins: 25% (Year 3)
- Customer LTV: ₹1.5L | LTV:CAC Ratio: 18.75x

## Assumptions and Methodology

### Key Assumptions
**Market Assumptions:**
- Indian SME market: 60M businesses
- Addressable market: 8M businesses (₹10L-₹10Cr revenue)
- Market penetration: 1.25% by Year 3 (100K customers)
- Average customer lifespan: 6 years

**Pricing Assumptions:**
- Free tier conversion: 15% to paid plans
- Average revenue per user: ₹25K annually
- Churn rate: 5% annually (20% in Year 1)
- Enterprise premium: 20% of customers

**Cost Assumptions:**
- Gross margins: 75% (SaaS business model)
- Sales efficiency: ₹8K CAC with 18.75x LTV:CAC
- Operating leverage: 60% variable costs
- Tax rate: 22% (including surcharge and cess)

### Projection Methodology
**Bottom-Up Modeling:**
- Customer acquisition based on marketing spend
- Revenue projections from customer cohorts
- Cost structure based on headcount and infrastructure
- Cash flow projections with working capital requirements

**Conservative Approach:**
- 70% of optimistic scenario assumptions
- 6-month lag in customer acquisition ramp
- Higher-than-industry churn rates
- Contingency reserves for risks
