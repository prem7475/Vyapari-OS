# Vyapari OS Revenue Projections

## Assumptions
- Customer acquisition: 500/month in Year 1, doubling each year
- Churn rate: 5% monthly (60% annual)
- Average revenue per user (ARPU): ₹4,500/month
- Transaction fee revenue: 20% of subscription revenue
- Premium feature revenue: 10% of subscription revenue
- Operating expenses: 70% of revenue in Year 1, decreasing to 60% by Year 3

## Year 1 Projections (2025)

### Customer Growth
- Starting customers: 2,000
- Monthly acquisitions: 500
- Monthly churn: 5%
- Ending customers: 8,000
- Average customers: 5,000

### Revenue Breakdown
- Subscription revenue: ₹270M (₹4,500 × 5,000 × 12)
- Transaction fees: ₹54M (20% of subscription)
- Premium features: ₹27M (10% of subscription)
- **Total revenue: ₹351M**

### Costs

### Profitability

- Net profit: ₹88M (25% margin)
