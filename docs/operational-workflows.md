# Vyapari OS Operational Workflows

## Executive Summary

Vyapari OS operational workflows define the core business processes, team structures, and operational procedures required to deliver our AI-powered business automation platform to Indian SMEs. Our workflows ensure efficient customer acquisition, successful onboarding, ongoing support, and continuous product improvement.

**Operational Objectives:**
- 99.9% platform uptime and reliability
- 24-hour average response time for support tickets
- 95% customer satisfaction score
- 30-day average time-to-value for new customers
