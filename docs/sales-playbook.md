# Vyapari OS Sales Playbook

## Executive Summary

Vyapari OS sales playbook provides a comprehensive framework for our sales organization to effectively sell AI-powered business automation to Indian SMEs. Our sales strategy combines digital inbound leads, strategic partnerships, and direct sales efforts to achieve 10,000 customers and ₹15 Cr revenue in Year 1.

**Sales Objectives:**
- 10,000 customers acquired in Year 1
- ₹15 Cr annual recurring revenue
- ₹8,000 average customer acquisition cost
- 6-8 week average sales cycle

## Target Customer Segmentation

### Primary Segments
**Small Businesses (₹10L-₹2Cr revenue):**
- Target: 6M businesses
- Deal Size: ₹599-₹1,499/month
- Sales Cycle: 4-6 weeks
- Decision Maker: Business owner
- Buying Triggers: Compliance pressure, growth needs

**Micro Enterprises (₹2L-₹10L revenue):**
- Target: 2M businesses
- Deal Size: Free to ₹599/month
- Sales Cycle: 2-4 weeks
- Decision Maker: Individual owner
- Buying Triggers: Basic digitization, tax filing

### Secondary Segments
**Medium Enterprises (₹2Cr-₹10Cr revenue):**
- Target: 0.8M businesses
- Deal Size: ₹1,499-₹4,999/month
- Sales Cycle: 8-12 weeks
- Decision Maker: Senior management
- Buying Triggers: Scaling operations, advanced analytics

## Sales Process Framework

### Sales Funnel Stages
**Stage 1: Lead Generation (TOFU)**
- Inbound: Digital marketing, content, SEO
- Outbound: Cold outreach, networking
- Partners: Referral programs, co-selling
- Target: 50K monthly qualified leads

**Stage 2: Lead Qualification (MOFU)**
- BANT criteria evaluation
- Lead scoring and prioritization
- Sales Qualified Lead (SQL) handoff
- Target: 10K monthly SQLs

**Stage 3: Discovery & Demo (BOFU)**
- Initial conversation and needs assessment
- Product demo and value proposition
- Objection handling and solution mapping
- Target: 5K monthly demos

**Stage 4: Proposal & Negotiation**
- Custom proposal development
- Pricing discussion and negotiation
- Contract terms and implementation planning
- Target: 2K monthly proposals

**Stage 5: Closing & Onboarding**
- Final agreement and payment processing
- Customer onboarding coordination
- Handover to customer success team
- Target: 1K monthly closes

### Sales Cycle Management
**Average Sales Cycle: 6-8 weeks**
- Lead to SQL: 1-2 weeks
- SQL to Demo: 2-3 weeks
- Demo to Proposal: 1-2 weeks
- Proposal to Close: 2-4 weeks

**Cycle Time Optimization:**
- Automated lead routing and assignment

## Sales Qualification Framework

### BANT Qualification Criteria
**Budget (B):**
- Can they afford Vyapari OS?
- What is their current software spend?
- Do they have budget approval authority?

**Authority (A):**
- Are they the decision maker?
- Who else is involved in the decision?
- What is their level of influence?

**Need (N):**
- What business problems are they facing?
- How critical is digital transformation to them?
- What solutions have they considered?

**Timeline (T):**
- When do they need to implement?
- What is driving their timeline?
- Are there regulatory deadlines?

### Lead Scoring System
**Demographic Score (0-20 points):**
- Business size and revenue: 10 points
- Industry alignment: 5 points
- Geographic location: 5 points

**Behavioral Score (0-50 points):**
- Website engagement: 15 points
- Content downloads: 10 points
- Email opens/clicks: 10 points
- Demo requests: 15 points

**Qualification Score (0-30 points):**
- BANT criteria met: 15 points
- Budget confirmed: 10 points
- Timeline defined: 5 points

**Total Score Thresholds:**
- Hot Lead (>70 points): Immediate follow-up
- Warm Lead (40-70 points): Nurture sequence
- Cold Lead (<40 points): Long-term nurture

## Discovery and Demo Process

### Initial Discovery Call
**Call Structure (45 minutes):**
1. **Introduction (5 min):** Rapport building, agenda setting
2. **Company Overview (10 min):** Customer background, business challenges
3. **Pain Point Exploration (15 min):** Current processes, specific problems
4. **Solution Discussion (10 min):** How Vyapari OS addresses their needs
5. **Next Steps (5 min):** Demo scheduling, information gathering

**Key Discovery Questions:**
- What are your biggest operational challenges?
- How much time do you spend on manual processes?
- What compliance requirements are you dealing with?
- What software tools are you currently using?
- What are your growth plans for the next 12 months?

### Product Demo Framework
**Demo Structure (60 minutes):**
1. **Context Setting (5 min):** Business problem recap
2. **Platform Overview (10 min):** Vyapari OS capabilities
3. **Feature Deep Dive (30 min):** Customized to customer needs
4. **ROI Discussion (10 min):** Value proposition and benefits
5. **Q&A and Next Steps (5 min):** Address concerns, timeline discussion

**Demo Customization:**
- Industry-specific workflows
- Pain point-focused feature showcase
- Competitor comparison
- Pricing discussion integration

### Objection Handling
**Common Objections and Responses:**

**"We're too small for this software":**
- "Vyapari OS is designed specifically for businesses like yours. Our free tier lets you start small and grow with your business."

**"We already have accounting software":**
- "Vyapari OS integrates with your existing tools and provides comprehensive business management beyond just accounting."

**"The pricing is too high":**
- "Let's calculate the ROI. How much time and money do you spend on manual processes? Vyapari OS typically pays for itself within 3-6 months."

**"We're happy with our current processes":**
- "That's great to hear. However, with increasing compliance requirements and competition, digital transformation is becoming essential for business survival."

**"We need to check with our accountant/partner":**
- "I'd be happy to join that conversation. In fact, we have special partner pricing and can provide joint demos with your trusted advisors."

1. **Executive Summary:** Customer situation and recommended solution

3. **Solution Overview:** Vyapari OS features and benefits
4. **Implementation Plan:** Timeline and milestones
5. **Pricing and Terms:** Packages, discounts, payment terms
6. **ROI Analysis:** Cost savings and business benefits
