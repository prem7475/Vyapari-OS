# Vyapari OS Refund Policy

## Last Updated: December 2024

Vyapari OS is committed to providing high-quality business management solutions. This Refund Policy outlines our refund procedures, eligibility criteria, and processing guidelines. All refunds are processed in accordance with applicable laws and our commitment to customer satisfaction.

## 1. Refund Eligibility

### 1.1 General Refund Policy
Vyapari OS offers refunds under the following circumstances:
- **Service Unavailability:** Extended service outages or technical issues
- **Billing Errors:** Incorrect charges or duplicate billing
- **Account Termination:** Voluntary account closure within refund window
- **Service Dissatisfaction:** Within the satisfaction guarantee period

### 1.2 Refund Windows
- **Free Trial Period:** Full refund available during 14-day trial
- **Monthly Subscriptions:** Refund available within 7 days of billing
- **Annual Subscriptions:** Pro-rated refund within 30 days of billing
- **Enterprise Contracts:** Custom terms as per contract agreement

### 1.3 Non-Refundable Items
The following are generally not eligible for refunds:
- **Partial Month Usage:** Unused portion of billing cycle
- **Third-Party Services:** Charges from integrated third-party services
- **Custom Development:** Bespoke features or custom integrations
- **Training Services:** Completed training sessions or workshops
- **Domain Registration:** Third-party domain registration fees

## 2. Refund Types and Processing

### 2.1 Full Refunds
Full refunds are available for:
- **Trial Period Cancellation:** Within 14 days of account creation
- **Service Unavailability:** Downtime exceeding 99% uptime SLA
- **Billing Errors:** Incorrect charges exceeding ₹1,000
- **Account Closure:** Within 48 hours of first payment

### 2.2 Partial Refunds
Partial refunds are calculated as:
- **Pro-rated Amount:** Based on unused service period
- **Usage-Based:** Reflecting actual service consumption
- **Discounted Amount:** After applying early termination fees
- **Adjusted Amount:** For plan downgrades or feature changes

### 2.3 Refund Methods
Refunds are processed through:
- **Original Payment Method:** Credit/debit card or UPI reversal
- **Bank Transfer:** Direct bank transfer for high-value refunds
- **Account Credit:** Future billing credit for subscription adjustments
- **Gift Cards:** Digital vouchers for specific refund scenarios

## 3. Refund Request Process

### 3.1 Initiating Refund Requests
To request a refund:
- **Contact Support:** Email refund@vyaparios.com or use in-app support
- **Provide Details:** Account information, payment details, reason for refund
- **Submit Documentation:** Screenshots, error logs, or supporting evidence
- **Specify Amount:** Requested refund amount and preferred method

### 3.2 Refund Review Process
Our refund review includes:
- **Initial Assessment:** 24-48 hours for basic refund requests
- **Detailed Review:** Up to 7 business days for complex cases
- **Evidence Evaluation:** Review of provided documentation and usage data
- **Approval Decision:** Based on eligibility criteria and policy compliance

### 3.3 Refund Processing Time
- **Standard Processing:** 7-14 business days from approval
- **Expedited Processing:** 2-3 business days for urgent cases
- **Bank Transfer Time:** Additional 3-5 business days for bank processing
- **Credit Card Reversals:** 3-5 business days for card issuer processing

## 4. Specific Refund Scenarios

### 4.1 Trial Period Refunds
- **Eligibility:** Within 14 days of account creation
- **Refund Amount:** 100% of any charges incurred
- **Processing:** Immediate upon request approval
- **Conditions:** No usage restrictions or minimum usage requirements

### 4.2 Subscription Cancellation Refunds
- **Monthly Plans:** Pro-rated refund for unused portion
- **Annual Plans:** Pro-rated refund minus 10% early termination fee
- **Enterprise Plans:** As per contract terms and conditions
- **Auto-renewal:** Refund available within 7 days of unwanted renewal

### 4.3 Service Issue Refunds
- **Downtime Credits:** Service credits for uptime below SLA
- **Bug Refunds:** Partial refunds for critical functionality issues
- **Integration Failures:** Refunds for third-party integration problems
- **Data Loss:** Compensation for service-caused data loss

### 4.4 Billing Error Refunds
- **Duplicate Charges:** 100% refund for duplicate billing
- **Incorrect Amounts:** Full refund for overcharging
- **Failed Transactions:** Refund for failed payment processing
- **Currency Errors:** Adjustment for incorrect currency conversion

## 5. Refund Conditions and Limitations

### 5.1 Usage-Based Restrictions
Refunds may be limited based on:
- **Data Volume:** Excessive data storage or processing usage
- **API Calls:** High-volume API usage beyond plan limits
- **User Accounts:** Multiple user accounts or team member additions
- **Custom Features:** Usage of premium or custom functionality

### 5.2 Time Limitations
- **Request Window:** Refund requests must be within specified timeframes
- **Processing Deadline:** No refunds processed after 90 days from billing
- **Account Age:** Refunds not available for accounts older than 12 months
- **Previous Refunds:** Limited to one refund per billing cycle

### 5.3 Abuse Prevention
We reserve the right to deny refunds for:
- **Policy Violations:** Breach of terms of service or acceptable use
- **Fraudulent Activity:** Suspected fraudulent or abusive usage
- **Chargebacks:** After payment processor chargeback initiation
- **Multiple Requests:** Excessive refund requests from same account
